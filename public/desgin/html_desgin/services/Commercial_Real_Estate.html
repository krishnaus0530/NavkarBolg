Commercial Real Estate FAQs

What's the typical lease term for commercial properties?

Commercial leases typically range from 3-10 years, with options for renewal. We negotiate favorable terms based on your business needs.

How do you determine commercial property values?

We use multiple methods including income approach, cost approach, and comparative market analysis specific to commercial properties.

What due diligence is needed for commercial purchases?

Includes title search, zoning verification, environmental assessments, structural inspections, and financial analysis of existing leases.

Can you help with commercial property financing?

Yes, we work with multiple lenders and can help secure commercial mortgages, construction loans, and bridge financing.
