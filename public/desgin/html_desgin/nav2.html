  • Water sports including kayaking and paddleboarding

Essential items to pack for your tropical getaway:

  • Lightweight clothing and swimwear
  • Sun protection (sunscreen, hat, sunglasses)
  • Comfortable walking shoes for excursions
  • Underwater camera for snorkeling
  • Travel adapters and necessary medications
  • Beach bag and reading materials

Don't miss out on these local delicacies:

  • Fresh seafood: Daily catch prepared in traditional style
  • Tropical fruits: Mango, pineapple, papaya, and exotic varieties
  • Local specialties: Coconut-based dishes and grilled meats
  • Street food: Experience the local food markets

We can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.
